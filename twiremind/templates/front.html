TwiRemind.Me

Reminders delivered to your Twitter Inbox!


What is it?
TwiRemind.Me is a simple service that allows you to schedule reminders in the future. These reminders are then delivered directly to your twitter inbox when the time comes.
Why Twitter Direct Message?
Because Twitter delivers the message through a number of media - including email, web, different desktop/mobile clients and most importantly through SMS. You can totally control how you want to get the reminder.
Why not just use a Calendar Service?
Calendar services are very often better suited. However, with Twitter DM, you can attach a custom 140 chars message. You are in the control of what is sent in the message.
